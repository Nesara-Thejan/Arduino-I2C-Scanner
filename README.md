This is a I2C scanner for arduino.Connect the pins of the component to the Arduino correctly. VCC to 5V  |  GND to GND | SCL to SCL pin in the Aduino | SDA to SDA in the Aduino.SDA and SCL pins are different to each Arduino board. For Aduino UNO SCL is A5 SDA is A4 , For Aduino Mega SCL  and SDA pins are at the top right of the GPIO pin rail.I have attached three pinout diagrams of Arduino UNO, MEGA, NANO. If you want to know more about these boards or wnat know pinouts of the other Arduino boards you can visit Official Arduino website.The link is below.   https://docs.arduino.cc/hardware
